FAQ

how to cancel booking?

To cancel your booking, Log into your Rentloze account and navigate to your bookings dashboard. select the relevant booking and choose the 'cancel' option. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the property, so be sure to review the terms before confirming your cancellation.

when will I get the possession?

possession details are outlined in the rental agreement. Typically, possession is granted once the payment is confirmed and the rental agreement is signed. You will receive a confirmation via email detailing the possession date and instructions for accessing the property.

where can I pay the rent?

Rent payment can be made directly through your Rentloze account. Navigate to the payment section, select your property, and follow the prompts to securely process your payment, We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

how to contact with the buyers?

To contact the buyer, log into your Rentloze account and go to your listings.Click on the specific property listing to access the contact details of the buyer. you can reach out via the provided messaging system or directly use the contact information listed.

why my listing not showing up?

If your listings are not showing up, check that they have been published and are not marked as 'Draft' Additionally, ensure all required fields are completed and no violations of our site policies exist. If the issue persists, Please reach out to our support team for assistance.

how to promote my listing?

To promote your listing on rentloze, consider upgrading to featured status for enhanced visibility. you can also share your listing on social media platforms, engage with potential renters through the messaging features, and ensure your listing has high-quality images and an appealing description to attract more interest.
